ANTI WRINKLE


Over time, your skin’s natural ability to protect itself reduces. Muscle contractions and movement leave their mark. Anti-wrinkle injections are a non-invasive, quick, affordable and effective way to help reduce and prevent wrinkle formation. By injecting a purified protein such as Botox or Dysport the underlying muscles relax and static lines and wrinkles are erased.
